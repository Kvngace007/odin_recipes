Jollof Rice

How To Make Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of long-grain rice
  • 3-4 Red bell pepper (Tatashe)
  • 2 Scotch bonnet pepper (Ata-rodo)
  • Foil wrapper
  • 3 medium-sized tomatoes
  • 2 sachets of tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp of Cooking oil
  • Beef/chicken stock
  • Butter (optional)
  • 1 large red onions
  • White pepper to taste
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp of Ginger (ground)
  • 1 tbsp of Curry powder
  • 1 tbsp of thyme
  • Paprika to taste
  • Seasoning cube(s) to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • Other spices like ground nutmeg, black pepper (Adds to your taste but it’s entirely optional).

Steps

  1. After broiling your red bell pepper (tatashe), scotch bonnet pepper (Ata-rodo), onions, garlic and ginger in the oven; you blend. Then you put into a pot and boil till all the water is absorbed and you’re left with a thick paste.
  2. Perboil your rice.
  3. Get your stainless steel pot and put in your cooking oil and butter.
  4. Sauté your onions till it’s fragrant and translucent.
  5. Add your tomato paste and stir while frying.
  6. Add thyme, curry, paprika, white pepper. Keep stirring while frying.
  7. Then you add your blended tomato mix and a little of your beef/chicken stock.
  8. After adding the stock, check for seasoning and add your salt, seasoning cube if needed.
  9. take out a little of your stew base from the pot and then add your rice.
  10. Stir continuously till all the grains have been coated in the stew.
  11. Then, add your stock or water to exactly the level of your rice.
  12. Cover your pot and allow to cook in low to medium heat till all the liquid has been absorbed. This can usually take about 20-30mins.
  13. Then cover your pot in a foil wrapper and close with a tight lid. Let the steam do the rest of the work.
  14. Check for doneness, garnish and serve
